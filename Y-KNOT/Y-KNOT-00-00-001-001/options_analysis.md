# Options Analysis

## Y-KNOT-00-00-001-001: General Knowledge Gap Resolution

### Overview

This document evaluates the available options for resolving the knowledge gaps identified in O-KNOT-00-00-001.

### Option 1: Systematic Heritage Review

**Description:** Conduct comprehensive review of all heritage documentation and systematically identify gaps.

**Pros:**
- Complete coverage of existing knowledge
- Clear traceability to sources
- Supports certification evidence

**Cons:**
- Resource intensive
- Extended timeline
- May identify additional gaps

### Option 2: Risk-Based Prioritization

**Description:** Focus resolution efforts on highest-impact knowledge gaps first.

**Pros:**
- Efficient resource utilization
- Faster initial results
- Addresses critical gaps early

**Cons:**
- May miss lower-priority gaps
- Requires accurate risk assessment
- Iterative refinement needed

### Option 3: Hybrid Approach

**Description:** Combine systematic review with risk-based prioritization.

**Pros:**
- Balanced approach
- Addresses critical gaps while ensuring coverage
- Scalable execution

**Cons:**
- More complex to manage
- Requires clear governance
- Dual tracking needed

### Recommendation

Option 3 (Hybrid Approach) is recommended as the preferred resolution path.
