# ATA 00 — General

**ATA Chapter:** 00  
**Title:** General  
**OPT-IN Axis:** [O-ORGANIZATIONS](../)  
**Scope:** Program-wide terminology, conventions, and cross-reference systems  

---

## 1. Overview

ATA Chapter 00 establishes foundational conventions for the AMPEL360 Q100 program. All other ATA chapters reference Chapter 00 for:

- **Terminology** — Standardized definitions for novel Q100 concepts
- **Document Architecture** — Numbering, versioning, and navigation conventions
- **Safety Labeling** — Warning, caution, and note standards including H₂-specific symbols
- **Cross-References** — Traceability system linking requirements to certification evidence
- **Unit Systems** — SI/imperial conventions with H₂-specific unit definitions
