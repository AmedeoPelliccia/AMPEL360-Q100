## 1. European Green Deal

**Target:** Climate-neutral Europe by 2050

**Aviation Contribution Required:**
- **55% CO₂ reduction by 2030** (vs. 1990)
- **NetZero aviation by 2050**

**Q100 Alignment:**
- H₂ PEM fuel cells → zero emissions
- BWB → 30%+ aerodynamic efficiency
- Distributed propulsion → noise reduction
