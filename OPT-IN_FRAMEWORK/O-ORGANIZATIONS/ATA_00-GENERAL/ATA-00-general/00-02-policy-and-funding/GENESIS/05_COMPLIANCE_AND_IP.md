# 05_COMPLIANCE_AND_IP — Export Control & Dissemination

**Purpose:** IP management and compliance requirements  
**Status:** Authoritative

---

## 1. Intellectual Property

**Default:** Background IP remains with partners  
**Generated IP:** Shared under consortium agreement  
**TEKTOKs:** Govern IP use and exploitation

---

## 2. Export Control

**Applicable:** Dual-use technologies (EU Regulation 2021/821)  
**Sensitive Areas:**
- H₂ propulsion systems (potential dual-use)
- AI/ML flight control (ITAR considerations)

**Compliance:** Export classification documented per ATA

---

## 3. Dissemination

**Open:** Publications, public deliverables  
**Restricted:** Proprietary designs, test data (partners only)  
**Confidential:** ITAR/export-controlled content
