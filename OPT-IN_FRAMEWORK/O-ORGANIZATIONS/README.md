# O-ORGANIZATIONS (ATA 00–05)

**Axis:** O — Organizations  
**ATA Scope:** Chapters 00–05  
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-27

---

## Overview

The O-ORGANIZATIONS axis covers general administrative and organizational documentation, including maintenance policy, operations organization, support information, airworthiness limitations, and time limits for maintenance checks.

---

| ATA | Title | Description |
|-----|-------|-------------|

| [01](./ATA_01-MAINTENANCE_POLICY/) | Maintenance Policy | Maintenance program policy and philosophy |

---

## Key Relationships

- **ATA 00** provides the foundation for all other chapters
- **ATA 04** (AWLs) feeds into maintenance planning across all systems
- **ATA 05** establishes the maintenance check intervals used by LC13 (MRO)
