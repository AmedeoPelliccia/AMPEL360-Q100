## Strategic Purpose

This system provides comprehensive traceability and digital product passport capabilities for the AMPEL360 Q100 aircraft program, enabling lifecycle transparency, regulatory compliance, and circular economy principles.

---

## Mission Statement

Establish and maintain a comprehensive Digital Product Passport (DPP) and traceability ledger system that ensures complete lifecycle visibility, regulatory compliance, and circular economy enablement for all AMPEL360 Q100 components and systems.
