# 96-50 — Audit Evidence Packs & Digital Signoffs

**Parent:** ATA-96-traceability-dpp-ledger  
**Section Type:** Compliance & Verification  
**Status:** Active Development

---

## Overview

This section manages audit evidence collection, packaging, and digital signoff workflows. It ensures regulatory compliance through comprehensive documentation and cryptographic proof of approvals.

---

## Purpose

- **Evidence Packs**: Structured collections of compliance artifacts
- **Digital Signatures**: Cryptographic proof of approval and authorization
- **Audit Trails**: Complete history of reviews, approvals, and changes
- **Compliance Proof**: Demonstrable adherence to regulations and standards
- **Signoff Workflows**: Automated approval routing and tracking

---

## Key Components

### 1. Evidence Pack Structure
- Evidence manifest (list of included artifacts)
- Supporting documentation (specifications, test reports, analyses)
- Traceability links (requirements, designs, tests)
- Metadata (timestamps, authors, versions)
- Hash values for integrity verification

### 2. Digital Signature Framework
- PKI infrastructure integration
- Qualified electronic signatures (eIDAS compliant)
- Multi-party signature workflows
- Signature verification services
- Certificate authority integration

### 3. Audit Trail Management
- Complete change history logging
- Review and approval records
- Comment and issue tracking
- Automated notifications and reminders
- Archive and retention policies

### 4. Compliance Demonstration
- Regulatory requirement mapping
- Evidence completeness checking
- Gap analysis and remediation
- Compliance reports generation
- Authority submission packages
