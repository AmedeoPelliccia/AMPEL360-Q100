## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose

This document specifies the system-level requirements for the AMPEL360 Q100 Liquid Hydrogen (LH₂) Fuel Storage System (ATA 28-10). These requirements define the functional, performance, interface, safety, and environmental characteristics required for safe and effective operation.

### 1.2 Scope

This specification applies to:
- Primary LH₂ storage tank assembly
- Tank support structure and mounting
- Thermal insulation system
- Fill, drain, and vent interfaces
- Level sensing and instrumentation

### 1.3 Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **LH₂** | Liquid Hydrogen at cryogenic temperatures (20-70 K) |
| **GH₂** | Gaseous Hydrogen at ambient or elevated temperatures |
| **MLI** | Multi-Layer Insulation |
| **COPV** | Composite Overwrap Pressure Vessel |
| **Boil-off** | Evaporation of LH₂ due to heat ingress |
| **Gravimetric Efficiency** | Ratio of fuel mass to total tank system mass |

## 2. Functional Requirements

### 2.1 Storage Function

| ID | Requirement | Verification |
|----|-------------|--------------|
| REQ-28-10-FUN-001 | The tank system shall safely contain LH₂ at operating pressures from 2 to 5 bar absolute | Test, Analysis |
| REQ-28-10-FUN-002 | The tank system shall safely contain LH₂ at temperatures from 20 K to 70 K | Test, Analysis |
| REQ-28-10-FUN-003 | The tank system shall provide controlled filling from ground servicing equipment | Demonstration |
| REQ-28-10-FUN-004 | The tank system shall provide controlled draining for maintenance | Demonstration |
| REQ-28-10-FUN-005 | The tank system shall provide pressure relief to prevent over-pressurization | Test |
| REQ-28-10-FUN-006 | The tank system shall provide safe venting of boil-off gas | Test, Analysis |

### 2.2 Fuel Delivery Function

| ID | Requirement | Verification |
|----|-------------|--------------|
| REQ-28-10-FUN-010 | The tank system shall deliver LH₂ to the fuel distribution system at required flow rate | Test |
| REQ-28-10-FUN-011 | The tank system shall maintain fuel delivery during all normal flight attitudes | Analysis, Test |
| REQ-28-10-FUN-012 | The tank system shall support fuel transfer between tanks for CG management | Demonstration |

## 3. Performance Requirements

### 3.1 Capacity and Efficiency

| ID | Requirement | Value | Rationale |
|----|-------------|-------|-----------|
| REQ-28-10-PER-001 | Gravimetric Efficiency | ≥ 6.0% | Competitive range performance |
| REQ-28-10-PER-002 | Volumetric Efficiency | ≥ 40 kg/m³ | BWB volume constraints |
| REQ-28-10-PER-003 | Usable Fuel Fraction | ≥ 98% | Mission fuel availability |
| REQ-28-10-PER-004 | Total LH₂ Capacity | 800 ± 20 kg | Mission range requirement |

### 3.2 Thermal Performance

| ID | Requirement | Value | Rationale |
|----|-------------|-------|-----------|
| REQ-28-10-PER-010 | Ground Boil-off Rate | ≤ 0.5%/day | Ground operations efficiency |
| REQ-28-10-PER-011 | Flight Boil-off Rate | ≤ 0.1%/hour | Mission fuel budget |
| REQ-28-10-PER-012 | Max Heat Leak | ≤ 50 W total | Boil-off management |
| REQ-28-10-PER-013 | Insulation Integrity | No degradation over 30,000 cycles | Service life |

### 3.3 Operational Performance

| ID | Requirement | Value | Rationale |
|----|-------------|-------|-----------|
| REQ-28-10-PER-020 | Refuel Time (0-100%) | ≤ 30 minutes | Airline turnaround |
| REQ-28-10-PER-021 | Drain Time (100-0%) | ≤ 45 minutes | Maintenance |
| REQ-28-10-PER-022 | Ground Hold Time | ≥ 4 hours (no refuel) | Delay tolerance |

### 3.4 Structural Performance

| ID | Requirement | Value | Rationale |
|----|-------------|-------|-----------|
| REQ-28-10-PER-030 | Design Ultimate Pressure | 7.5 bar | 1.5× max operating |
| REQ-28-10-PER-031 | Proof Pressure | 6.25 bar | 1.25× max operating |
| REQ-28-10-PER-032 | Burst Pressure | ≥ 10 bar | Certification margin |
| REQ-28-10-PER-033 | Cycle Life | ≥ 30,000 cycles | Aircraft service life |
| REQ-28-10-PER-034 | Damage Tolerance | No leak before burst | Safe-life/fail-safe |

## 5. Safety Requirements

### 5.1 Hazard Prevention

| ID | Requirement | Safety Objective |
|----|-------------|------------------|
| REQ-28-10-SAF-001 | Tank failure leading to uncontained H₂ release shall be ≤ 10⁻⁹ per flight hour | Catastrophic |
| REQ-28-10-SAF-002 | H₂ leak detection shall trigger crew alerting within 30 seconds | Hazardous |
| REQ-28-10-SAF-003 | Automatic isolation shall prevent H₂ flow to damaged sections | Hazardous |
| REQ-28-10-SAF-004 | Pressure relief shall prevent tank rupture under all conditions | Catastrophic |

### 5.2 Fire Protection

| ID | Requirement | Verification |
|----|-------------|--------------|
| REQ-28-10-SAF-010 | Tank zone shall be classified as Zone 2 per CS 25.863 | Analysis |
| REQ-28-10-SAF-011 | Ignition sources shall be eliminated from tank zone | Inspection |
| REQ-28-10-SAF-012 | Fire detection coverage per CS 25.858 | Test |
