# E2-ENERGY

**Energy Systems (ATA 24, 49)**

This axis of the OPT-IN Framework covers **energy generation and management systems** for the AMPEL360 Q100 aircraft — electrical power generation, distribution, and auxiliary power systems.

> **Governance Note:** ATA chapters 24 and 49 are standard ATA chapters for Energy Systems. They represent *"what generates and manages energy"* — electrical power, APU, and energy management.

---

## ATA Chapters in This Axis

| Chapter | System | Description |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| ATA 24 | Electrical Power | AC/DC generation, distribution, external power |
| ATA 49 | Airborne Auxiliary Power (APU) | Auxiliary power unit systems |

---

## Operational Rule

> **E2-ENERGY (T-TECHNOLOGIES / energy)**: *"lo que genera y gestiona energía"* → electrical power, APU, energy storage, power distribution.

If the artifact is **energy/power generation or distribution** → place in **E2-ENERGY**.
